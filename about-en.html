About

Budapest, pretty girls and goulash... these are the most common things people mention when they amicably start chatting to us on a plane back to Budapest or when they casually talk to us on a meetup and learn that we are originally from Hungary. This is all great but we've always felt that something is missing: what about the people and great achievements of today's Hungary? Why does no one know about that?

Flóra and Zsolt, the creators of this website


We believe that better showcasing the creative business ideas, ingenious arts and crafts that Hungarian entrepreneurs create, will help us add a new cliché to the well-known ones, something like this: "creative and talented Hungarians". And, as a big plus, it gives you the opportunity to find local suppliers and products more easily thus shop more sustainably.


Browse & learn about Hungarian entrepreneurs and their ingenious ideas and products. Help us expand this list: if you know of a similar great idea, fill out our form.
